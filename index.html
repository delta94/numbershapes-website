NumberShapes Whiteboard

We know that helping young learners with math is tough. That's why we created the NumberShapes Whiteboard. It's an easy and quick way to start conversation about math with your child. Interactive visualizations make thinking about numbers easy and fun. Plus we're adding more more features all the time. This is the kind of tool that will grow with your student and be something you can trust to help them learn.

Fractions

There couldn't be a simpler way to start learning about fractions. This completely gesture driven interface gives you and your student the freedom to create any fraction you like and also study reduced or equivalent fractions.

Addition

A thorough understanding Number Lines is the foundation of Number Sense. Our intuitive and elegant interface can be used for a myriad of problem solving strategies. It's also a relaxing way to keep up with math facts and help students feel in control of their learning.

Decimals

Visualization is key to a solid understanding of decimals. Use this app to talk to your student about how many hundreths make a whole and demonstrate how they're added together.
